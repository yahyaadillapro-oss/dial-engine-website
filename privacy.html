Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th December 2025

DIAL ENGINE™ is an enterprise-grade sales automation center. We operate under strict data sovereignty principles, ensuring that proprietary lead databases and agent telemetry remain securely locked down within your authorized corporate ecosystem.

1. Data Sovereignty & Storage

We do not pool your CRM data on shared, publicly accessible web servers. All client records and provisioned terminal data are housed in a dedicated PostgreSQL Lead Vault configured specifically for your firm. This ensures your data is strictly yours and shielded from external analytics scraping.

2. Terminal Isolation Protocol

To protect the integrity of the master database, deployed agents operate on strict "Zero-Footprint" terminals. The automation logic runs locally. Agents never interact directly with the master database, completely eliminating the risk of mass data exports, unauthorized duplication, or internal theft.

3. Telemetry & Administrative Oversight

Our background infrastructure continuously monitors CRM interactions and transfer logs to ensure the smooth operation of large-scale agent floors. This telemetry is streamed asynchronously to a private logging interface strictly for administrative oversight and gamification. We do not sell, share, or analyze this telemetry for third-party marketing.

4. Total Data Eradication

We enforce total corporate control. Upon the termination or offboarding of any agent, the DIAL ENGINE™ system executes an automated cloud command to permanently delete the agent's assigned terminal and revoke access instantly. The data is destroyed at the endpoint, ensuring zero retention post-employment.
