Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th December 2025

DIAL ENGINE™ prioritizes the data sovereignty of its clients. We understand that in enterprise sales, data is your most valuable asset. Our architecture is explicitly designed to keep your information entirely within your control.

1. Absolute Data Ownership

We do not pool, store, or warehouse your CRM leads on external developer servers. All vaults, active terminals, and client records generated by the automation hub belong exclusively to the Licensee. You have total authority over your operational data.

2. Isolated Automation Logistics

Dial Engine functions as an operational bridge. When you execute commands to dispense leads or shuffle your vault, the logic processes those actions without requiring us to read, harvest, or monetize the contact information within the files.

3. Telemetry & Gamification

To provide accurate leaderboards and floor analytics, the central hub tracks non-identifying telemetry (such as total daily dials, connection ratios, and aggregate transfer counts). This data is utilized solely for your firm's internal auditing and gamification. We do not sell or share this operational telemetry.

4. Secure Offboarding Protocols

The software provides tools designed to protect your data from internal leaks. When an agent is fired or offboarded via the central hub, the system cleanly revokes their access and secures the terminal, ensuring your leads remain strictly within your corporate environment.
