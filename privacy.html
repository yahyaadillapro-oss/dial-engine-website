Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

DIAL ENGINE™ is a native Google Drive CRM. We operate under a strict "Zero-Footprint" philosophy, ensuring your data never leaves your personal or corporate ecosystem.

1. Data Sovereignty

We do not host, store, or process your lead data on third-party backend servers. All property information from Zillow and lead details from your VOIP-ready sheets stay exclusively within your private Google Workspace. We have absolute zero access to your spreadsheets.

2. App Logic & Triggers

Our custom automation logic runs locally within your Google account's trusted environment. Features such as "Master KPI Injection" only transmit status updates between sheets you actively authorize. No external analytics databases are involved in core operations.

3. Enhanced Security

By eliminating the need for third-party browser extensions, DIAL ENGINE™ removes the severe risk of credential scraping, background screen recording, or passive data collection common in traditional SaaS CRMs.
