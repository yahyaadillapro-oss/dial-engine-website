Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th December 2025

DIAL ENGINE™ is an enterprise-grade sales infrastructure. We operate under strict data sovereignty principles, ensuring your proprietary lead data, PBX routing, and telecommunications telemetry remain securely locked down within your authorized ecosystem.

1. PostgreSQL Data Sovereignty

Unlike standard SaaS CRMs, we do not pool your leads on shared, publicly accessible web servers. All property information, custom lists, and client records are housed in a dedicated PostgreSQL Lead Vault. This vault is secured with advanced AES cryptography, meaning your data is strictly yours and completely shielded from external scraping.

2. Terminal Isolation Protocol

To protect your database, agents operate on strict "Zero-Footprint" Google Workspace terminals. App logic runs locally within your environment. Agents never interact directly with the master PostgreSQL vault, completely eliminating the risk of mass data exports, unauthorized duplication, or internal theft.

3. Telemetry & Administrative Oversight

Our background infrastructure continuously monitors SIP operations, terminal interactions, and transfer logs. This telemetry is stripped of non-essential metadata and streamed asynchronously to a private logging interface strictly for administrative oversight. We do not sell, share, or analyze this telemetry for third-party marketing.

4. Total Data Eradication

We enforce total corporate control. Upon the termination or offboarding of any agent, the DIAL ENGINE™ system instantly revokes all SIP credentials and executes a command via Google Drive APIs to permanently delete the agent's assigned terminal. The data is destroyed at the endpoint, ensuring zero retention post-employment.
